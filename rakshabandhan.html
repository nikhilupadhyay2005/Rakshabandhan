Rakshabandhan 2025

Nikhil Upadhyay — Software Engineer

Rakshabandhan is not merely a festival — it is an emotion, a bond that goes beyond bloodlines and embraces love, trust, and lifelong protection. On this auspicious occasion, sisters tie a rakhi, a sacred thread, on their brothers’ wrists, symbolizing their faith in him. And the brother, in turn, pledges to protect her with all his strength — not just physically, but emotionally, spiritually, and morally. As a Software Engineer, immersed in logic and code daily, I, Nikhil Upadhyay, find this festival a gentle reminder of the human values that power even the most advanced technologies — trust, connection, and care. In this digital age, where everything moves fast and virtual, the tradition of Rakhi brings back the warmth of human relationships and childhood me
